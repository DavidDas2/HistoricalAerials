Pictometry Widget

 
Close the Google Maps Widget.
 
Open the Pictometry Widget.
 
 
You will see this note:
 
Lat/Lon (None – please mouse over the map).
 
 
Move your mouse over the map until you see a Lat/Lon readout.
 
Hover your mouse over the Open Pictometry Link.
 
 
Make sure you see the Pictometry Link in the Status Bar below.
 
Click on the Open Pictometry Link.
 
This opens a separate Pictometry Window displaying high-resolution oblique imagery.
 
https://gis.maricopa.gov/pictometry/default.aspx?&qlat=33.44909&qlong=-112.09263
 
 
You can Rotate the image.
 
 
 
You can turn on the Streets, Contours and the Parcels layer.
 
 
 
You can zoom in and out.
 
 
You can view the imagery from a specific year.
 
Select 2016 Sep – 2016 Dec
 
 
There are 6 images available for 2016.
 
Press use the forward and back arrows to browse through these images.
