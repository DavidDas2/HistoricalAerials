Google Maps Widget

 
The Google Maps Widget launches a separate Google Maps window.
 
Refresh the Web App.
 
 
Turn off all the Map Layers.
 
 
Enter Parcel Number 112-02-143 in the Search Tool.
 
 
The map will zoom to the Arizona Department of Administration.
 
 
Turn on the Parcel Layer.
 
 
Open the Google Maps Widget.
 
 
Notice that it says:
 
Lat/Lon (None – please mouse over the map).
 
 
Move your mouse over the map until the Lat/Lon values begin to display.
 
 
Hover your mouse over the Open Google Maps Link.
 
 
Make sure the status bar at the bottom displays the link to Google Maps.
 
Click on the Open Google Maps Link.
 
 
This will open a separate Google Maps Window.
 
https://www.google.com/maps/@33.4487588,-112.0928446,193m/data=!3m1!1e3?entry=ttu
 
 
Activate the Google Pegman Tool.
 
All the streets having Street View information will appear in blue.
 
 
Hover your mouse over any spot on the blue street segments.
 
A thumbnail picture will appear.
 
 
Click on a spot on any blue street segment.
 
This will open the Google Street View window.
 
 
You can pan and zoom within this window and follow along the path markers.
 
 
Click on See More Dates.
 
 
Select May 2022
 
 
Close the Google Earth Window.
 
 
You can pick another spot.
 
 
Click on any Photo Sphere.
 
 
You can look around 360 degrees centered around this spot.
 
 
Close this window.
 
Hover your mouse over the Map Layers button.
 
 
Select More.
 
 
Turn on Globe View
 
Select the Satellite Imagery.
 
 
You are now ready to experience your world in Google 3D!
 
Hold down your Control Key while dragging your mouse over the map.
 
To change the Tilt, drag your mouse up or down while holding down the Control Key.
 
 
To change the Rotation, drag your mouse left or right while holding down the Control Key.
 
 
Zoom In by scrolling your mouse wheel forward.
 
 
Zoom Out by scrolling your mouse wheel backwards.
 
 
You may use the Compass Tool to Rotate the View in 90-degree increments heading North, East, South, West.
 
 
Clicking on the center of the Compass Tool will reset the view to face North.
 
Click on the 2D/3D toggle.
 
 
You are back to viewing your world in 2D.
 
 
Deactivate the Google Pegman Tool.
 
 
This will remove the Street View, Photo Path and Photo Sphere images.
