Coordinate Conversion Widget

 
Open the Coordinate Conversion Widget.
 
 
This widget lets you zoom to any Street Address or Coordinate.
 
Enter this Street Address:
 
301 W JEFFERSON ST
 
 
Select 301 W JEFFERSON ST, 85003 from the list of choices.
 
A red target will appear on the map at this address.
 
 
This widget will also display the coordinates of this Address Point in Decimal Degrees, Degree Minutes Seconds, and Longitude-Latitude.
 
Expand to see the coordinates.
 
 
Click on the Zoom Tool.
 
 
The map will zoom closer to your target location.
 
 
Click several times on the Zoom In Tool
