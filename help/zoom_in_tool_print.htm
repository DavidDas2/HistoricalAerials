Zoom In Tool

 
Press the Zoom In Tool once.
 
 
Notice how the map zooms in by one level.
 
 
Press the Zoom In Tool one more time.
 
The map zooms even closer.
 
 
There is a faster way to zoom in to your area of interest.
 
Hold down your <Shift> Key and then click on a point on the map with your left mouse button.
 
 
Do not release your left mouse button!
 
Drag your mouse to draw a rectangle.
 
 
Release your mouse.
 
The map will zoom to the extent defined by your rectangle.
 
 
On a tablet you may not have access to a mouse.
 
To zoom out, use two fingers to pinch out.
